Smart City Surveillance System

Surveillance System

Our Surveillance System is composed of Central Management or Monitoring which means that it can connect all the cameras and third party devices located at multiple locations or sites at a central hub. Through CMS, you can view, control, receive alerts & notifications from any camera or third party device, located anywhere in the system and take pro active decisions. Mobile video surveillance has gained immense popularity because of its adaptability which permits better security for buses, trucks and automobiles on public roads.

  • N+N/N+1 redundancy support for recording servers
