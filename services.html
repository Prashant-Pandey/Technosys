Services

Technosys provides security and survelliance systems services throughout India. We’ve been designing and implementing electronic security solutions for businesses of all sizes. While 21st century security challenges grow in complexity, our mission remains exactly the same: to protect against threats that cause harm. Our comprehensive services address the range of security needs. We provide world class services in order to transform India #makeinindia

CCTV Surveillance
System

Whether you’re responsible for the safety of employees and customers, reducing the threat of burglary, vandalism or liability claims, or a residential building manager accountable for protecting the grounds and tenants, video surveillance is crucial to help keeping your facility safe and secured.

Intelligent Traffic
Management System

Traffic Management Systems (TMS) provide permanent control across the network, automatically sets routes for trains and logs train movements as well as detects and solves potential conflicts. Technosys enable use of the effective functionality in local or regional systems up to nation-wide coverage.

Access Control
System
